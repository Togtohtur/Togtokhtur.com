Khalkhin Gol: History Education in Virtual Reality

Khalkhin-Gol is a Virtual Reality experience wherein you can view the events of one of the most important periods in Mongolian history. Utilising the Oculus Rift, users can walk through virtual museum and multiple environments. It is designed for students to take interest in, and learn more about history.

Tasks:
Development of assets
Programming interaction, UI, animation, trigger system for all environments, sound and animation

Software used: Zbrush, Autodesk Maya, Marvelous Designer, Photoshop, Premiere, After Effects

Interaction is implemented through the use of gaze detection, and events are either triggered through simple single-button usage, utilizing ray casting.
